# CSDC100PREFI
All About Ai website is is a simple and student-friendly website designed to help users understand Artificial Intelligence in a clear and responsible way. It provides easy-to-read explanations about Generative AI, ethical guidelines, examples of AI tools, common questions, and practical applications of AI in education and daily life. The website also includes articles, FAQs, and an analogy section to help users relate AI concepts to real-life situations. It aims to promote responsible and informed use of AI, especially for students in the academe.
