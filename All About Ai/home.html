What is Generative AI (GenAI)?

Generative AI, or GenAI, is a type of artificial intelligence that can create new content such as text, images, videos, music, and even code. Instead of just recognizing or analyzing information, GenAI learns patterns from large amounts of data and uses them to produce original outputs that look like they were made by a human.

Some common examples of GenAI tools include ChatGPT, Midjourney, Gemini, Claude, DALL·E, and AI video generators. These tools can help with writing, designing, brainstorming, studying, and many creative tasks.

GenAI is powerful, but it also needs responsible use. It can support learning and creativity, but users should always check accuracy, avoid plagiarism, and make sure the output is used ethically.
